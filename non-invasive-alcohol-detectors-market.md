<p><strong>The Non-invasive Alcohol Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhale-based</li><li>Touch-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-invasive alcohol detectors market is primarily divided into two types: exhale-based and touch-based. Exhale-based detectors analyze a person’s breath to measure alcohol concentration through breath samples, utilizing sensor technology for rapid results. Conversely, touch-based detectors assess alcohol levels through skin contact, often utilizing sensors that detect alcohol traces in sweat or skin temperature. Both types offer convenient alternatives to traditional breathalyzers, providing quick and user-friendly methods for alcohol detection without invasive procedures.</p></p>
