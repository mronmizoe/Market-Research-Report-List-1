<p><p>Ultrafast rectifier diodes are crucial components in power management systems, providing rapid switching and low forward voltage drop. The market is segmented into two main types: Single Common Anode and Dual Common Anode. Single Common Anode diodes feature one anode connection shared among multiple diodes, minimizing space and simplifying design. In contrast, Dual Common Anode diodes have two diodes sharing a common anode, enhancing efficiency in applications requiring higher voltage handling. Each type caters to specific circuit designs and performance needs.</p></p>
