<p><p>The milking claw market encompasses various types designed to facilitate efficient and hygienic milk extraction from dairy animals. Side-to-side milking claws allow for simultaneous milking of all four teats, enhancing workflow and comfort for both the animal and operator. In contrast, front-to-back milking claws prioritize a sequential milking process, often used in systems where space or cow behavior necessitates a different approach. Both types aim to optimize milking efficiency, reduce stress on the animals, and improve overall milk quality.</p></p>
