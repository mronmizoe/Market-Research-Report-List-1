<p><ul><li>Enrichment Approaches</li><li>Amplicon-Based Approaches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RNA Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) market is divided into two main types: enrichment approaches and amplicon-based approaches. Enrichment approaches involve selectively isolating specific RNA sequences before sequencing, enhancing sensitivity and enabling the study of rare transcripts. In contrast, amplicon-based approaches utilize PCR amplification to obtain specific RNA targets, allowing for focused analysis of regions of interest. Both methods facilitate comprehensive transcriptome analysis, aiding in disease research and personalized medicine by providing detailed insights into gene expression profiles.</p></p>
