<p><strong>The Telerobotics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Network Connections</li><li>Tethered Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telerobotics market is categorized into two main types based on connection methods: wireless network connections and tethered connections. Wireless network connections utilize internet or radio signals to operate remote robots, offering flexibility and ease of mobility across vast distances. In contrast, tethered connections involve physical cables that link the robot to a control unit, providing stable data transmission and power but limiting mobility. Both types cater to specific applications, balancing flexibility and reliability according to user needs.</p></p>
