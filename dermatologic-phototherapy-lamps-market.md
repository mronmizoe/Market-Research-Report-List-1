<p><p>The dermatologic phototherapy lamps market is divided into two primary types: local dermatologic phototherapy lamps and whole body dermatologic phototherapy lamps. Local lamps are designed for targeted treatment of specific skin conditions, allowing focused light application on affected areas, which is beneficial for conditions like psoriasis or eczema. In contrast, whole body lamps are used to treat widespread skin disorders, providing comprehensive exposure to ultraviolet light for conditions requiring broader treatment coverage, such as severe psoriasis or vitiligo.</p></p>
