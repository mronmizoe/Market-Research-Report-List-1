<p><strong>The Offshore Drilling Rigs Industry Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jackup Rigs</li><li>Semi-Submersible Rigs</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore drilling rigs market comprises three main types: jackup rigs, semi-submersible rigs, and drillships. Jackup rigs are mobile platforms supported by legs that elevate them above sea level, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersible rigs consist of buoyant structures that float on water, providing stability in deeper areas. Drillships are ships equipped for drilling that can navigate open waters, suitable for deepwater exploration. Each type caters to specific seabed conditions and water depths, influencing operational capabilities and costs.</p></p>
