<p><p>The thyroid ablation device market includes two primary types: radiofrequency ablation (RFA) and microwave ablation (MWA). RFA utilizes electrical currents to generate heat, effectively destroying thyroid tissue, particularly in cases of hyperthyroidism or nodules. MWA, on the other hand, employs microwave energy to achieve similar results, allowing for rapid tissue destruction with precise control and minimal damage to surrounding structures. Both techniques are minimally invasive alternatives, offering effective treatment options with reduced recovery times and improved patient comfort.</p></p>
