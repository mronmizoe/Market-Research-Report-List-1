<p><p>The aluminium ore market is categorized into two main types: open-air and underground. Open-air aluminium ore mining involves extracting bauxite from the surface, typically in large pits. This method is cost-effective and allows for the easy removal of overburden. In contrast, underground aluminium ore mining occurs below the earth's surface, requiring more complex operations and safety measures. While it can be more capital-intensive, this method is often used when surface deposits are exhausted or environmental concerns arise.</p></p>
