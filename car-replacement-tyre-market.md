<p><strong>The Car Replacement Tyre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car replacement tire market consists primarily of two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires feature layers of fabric that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, improved traction, and enhanced durability, making them the preferred choice for modern vehicles. In contrast, bias tires have layers that crisscross, providing a softer ride but generally lower performance and shorter lifespan. The choice between these types depends on the vehicle type and driving conditions, influencing consumer preferences and market dynamics.</p></p>
