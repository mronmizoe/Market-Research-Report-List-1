<p><p>The float switch market consists of three main types: top-mounted, side-mounted, and special types. Top-mounted float switches are installed on the tank's upper surface, making them ideal for applications with limited space. Side-mounted float switches are attached to the side of the tank, offering flexibility in installation and operation for varying liquid levels. Special type float switches are designed for specific applications, featuring customized designs or materials to meet unique industry requirements, enhancing versatility and functionality.</p></p>
