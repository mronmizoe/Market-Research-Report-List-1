<p><p>The skin grafting system market comprises various types of dermatomes used for harvesting skin grafts. Knife dermatomes are handheld instruments for precise graft cutting, while drum dermatomes feature rotating blades for consistent thickness. Electrical dermatomes utilize powered blades for efficiency and ease of use, allowing for rapid graft harvesting. Air-powered dermatomes operate with compressed air, providing quick and adjustable cutting capabilities. Each type offers distinct advantages in precision, speed, and ease of operation, catering to various clinical needs in skin grafting procedures.</p></p>
