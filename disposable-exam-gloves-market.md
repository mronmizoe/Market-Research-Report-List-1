<p><p>The disposable exam gloves market is categorized primarily into three types: latex, rubber, and nitrile. Latex gloves, made from natural rubber, offer excellent flexibility and tactile sensitivity but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Rubber gloves, typically made from synthetic materials, provide durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for various applications. Nitrile gloves are made from synthetic polymer, offering superior puncture resistance and hypoallergenic properties, making them increasingly popular in medical and industrial settings due to their versatility and safety profile.</p></p>
