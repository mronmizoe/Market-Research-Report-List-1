<p><strong>The Smart Payment Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact-based Smart Payment Cards</li><li>Contactless Smart Payment Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart payment cards are categorized into contact-based and contactless types. Contact-based smart payment cards require physical insertion into a reader for transactions, using chip technology to ensure secure data transmission. In contrast, contactless smart payment cards employ near-field communication (NFC) or radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing users to make payments by tapping their card on a reader. This quick and convenient method enhances user experience while maintaining transaction security, making contactless cards increasingly popular in the payment landscape.</p></p>
