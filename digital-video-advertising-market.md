<p><ul><li>In-stream Video Ad</li><li>Out-stream Video Ad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital video advertising market is primarily divided into two types: in-stream and out-stream video ads. In-stream video ads are embedded within streaming content, appearing before (pre-roll), during (mid-roll), or after (post-roll) the video. Out-stream video ads, on the other hand, are standalone video ads that automatically play within non-video environments, such as articles or social media feeds, engaging audiences without requiring video content as a backdrop. Both formats offer unique opportunities for targeting and engagement.</p></p>
