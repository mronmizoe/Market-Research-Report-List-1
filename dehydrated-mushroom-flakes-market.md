<p><strong>The Dehydrated Mushroom Flakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehydrated mushroom flakes market is primarily segmented into two processing types: air drying and freeze drying. Air drying involves removing moisture from mushrooms using warm air, resulting in a cost-effective method that preserves flavor but may affect texture. Freeze drying, on the other hand, involves freezing the mushrooms and then evaporating the ice under vacuum, retaining more nutrients and original texture, albeit at a higher cost. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and applications in food products.</p></p>
