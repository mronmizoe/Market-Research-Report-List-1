<p><p>The Redox Enzymes market encompasses various types, including catalase, glucose oxidase, and laccase, each serving distinct purposes in biochemical processes. Catalase facilitates the decomposition of hydrogen peroxide, protecting cells from oxidative damage. Glucose oxidase catalyzes the oxidation of glucose, widely utilized in food preservation and biosensing applications. Laccase is involved in the oxidation of phenolic compounds and is used in bioremediation and industrial processes. Other redox enzymes include peroxidases and dehydrogenases, broadening the market's applications across industries.</p></p>
