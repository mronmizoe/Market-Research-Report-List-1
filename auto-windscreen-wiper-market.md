<p><ul><li>Boneless Wipers</li><li>Bone Wipers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto windscreen wiper market features two main types: boneless wipers and bone wipers. Boneless wipers utilize a flexible, frameless design that allows for better conformity to the windshield, providing improved visibility and reduced streaking. In contrast, bone wipers consist of a traditional frame structure, which supports the wiping element and tends to be more robust. Both designs cater to different consumer preferences, with boneless wipers often favored for aesthetics and performance, while bone wipers are appreciated for their durability and reliability.</p></p>
