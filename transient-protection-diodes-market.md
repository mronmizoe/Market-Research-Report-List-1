<p><p>Transient protection diodes are crucial for safeguarding electronic circuits from voltage spikes. The market is segmented into uni-polar and bi-polar transient protection diodes. Uni-polar transient diodes protect against voltage surges in one direction, primarily used in DC applications. In contrast, bi-polar transient diodes can handle voltage spikes in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications. The choice between these types depends on the specific voltage protection requirements and the application environment, ensuring reliable operation in various electronic devices.</p></p>
