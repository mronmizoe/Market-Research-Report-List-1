<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Swing Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toothless Type</li><li>External Type</li><li>Internal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swing bearing market comprises three main types: toothless type, external type, and internal type. </p><p>The toothless type features a smooth surface for rotation without gear teeth, offering a compact design suitable for applications needing minimal maintenance. </p><p>The external type has gear teeth on the outer circumference, providing higher load capacity and stability, ideal for heavy machinery. </p><p>Conversely, the internal type features gear teeth on the inner circumference, facilitating precise movements and is commonly used in cranes and excavators for better control.</p></p>
