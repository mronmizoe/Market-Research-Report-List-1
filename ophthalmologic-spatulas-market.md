<p><p>The ophthalmologic spatulas market comprises single-ended and double-ended spatulas, each designed for specific surgical applications. In contrast, double-ended spatulas provide versatility with two working ends, allowing for various manipulations without needing additional instruments. The choice between them typically depends on the surgeon's preference and the specific procedure.</p></p>
