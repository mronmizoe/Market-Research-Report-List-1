<p><p>Photostimulable Phosphor (PSP) Systems are advanced imaging technologies used primarily in digital radiography. These systems utilize phosphor plates that store x-ray energy and subsequently release it as light when stimulated by a specific wavelength, allowing for the capture of high-quality images without the need for traditional film. PSP systems have gained popularity in various medical and industrial applications due to their capability to deliver quick imaging results and reduced radiation exposure.</p><p>The market for PSP Systems is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for efficient imaging modalities in healthcare and advancements in digital imaging technologies. The adoption of PSP systems is further propelled by their cost-effectiveness and the ability to retro-fit existing radiographic systems with digital capabilities. </p><p>The Photostimulable Phosphor (PSP) Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. Key trends shaping this market include the integration of artificial intelligence in radiography, the rise of point-of-care testing, and an expanding range of applications across various fields such as veterinary medicine, dental imaging, and industrial inspection. These trends indicate a robust evolution toward more reliable and versatile imaging solutions.</p></p>
