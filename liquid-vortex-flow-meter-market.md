<p><p>A Liquid Vortex Flow Meter is a device used to measure the flow rate of liquids by utilizing the principle of vortex shedding. When a fluid flows past a bluff body, vortices are generated and shed alternately from either side. The frequency of this vortex shedding is directly proportional to the flow velocity, allowing for accurate flow measurement.</p><p>The Liquid Vortex Flow Meter Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 15% during the forecast period.
