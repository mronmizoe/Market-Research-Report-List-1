<p><p>The photoplethysmograph (PPG) market is primarily categorized into single-channel and double-channel types. Single-channel PPG devices collect data from one site, making them ideal for simple monitoring tasks like heart rate tracking. In contrast, double-channel PPG devices can acquire data from two different sites simultaneously, providing more comprehensive insights into cardiovascular dynamics and variations in blood flow. This dual-channel capability enhances diagnostic accuracy, making double-channel devices increasingly popular in clinical and research applications.</p></p>
