<p><ul><li>Wall Mount Fiber Optic Junction Box</li><li>Rack Mount Fiber Optic Junction Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optic Junction Box market comprises two main types: wall mount and rack mount fiber optic junction boxes. Wall mount junction boxes are designed for installation on walls, providing a compact solution for managing optical fibers in limited spaces, often in smaller installations or residential settings. In contrast, rack mount junction boxes are designed to fit within standard server racks, offering a more expansive and organized solution for larger installations, typically in enterprise environments or data centers, facilitating easier access and management of fiber connections.</p></p>
