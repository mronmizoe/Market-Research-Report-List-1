<p><p>The Asset Integrity Management market encompasses various approaches aimed at ensuring the safety and reliability of assets throughout their lifecycle. Risk-Based Inspection (RBI) prioritizes inspections based on risk factors, while Reliability, Availability, and Maintainability (RAM) studies assess system performance. Corrosion Management focuses on preventing asset degradation, and Pipeline Integrity Management ensures pipeline safety. Hazard Identification (HAZID) studies identify potential risks, and Structural Integrity Management evaluates the strength of structures. Nondestructive Testing (NDT) Inspection employs techniques to assess material integrity without damage. Additional market segments address emerging needs and technologies in asset integrity.</p></p>
