<p><strong>The Offsite Manufactured Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Systems</li><li>Modular Systems</li><li>Panelised Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Offsite Manufactured Solution market encompasses three primary types: Volumetric Systems, Modular Systems, and Panelised Systems. Volumetric Systems involve pre-fabricated, fully functional units, typically complete with fixtures, which are transported and assembled on-site. Modular Systems consist of distinct, assembled sections that can be combined to create larger structures. Panelised Systems involve the fabrication of individual wall, floor, or roof panels that are transported and assembled on-site, allowing for flexible designs and quicker construction timelines.</p></p>
