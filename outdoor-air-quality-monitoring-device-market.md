<p><p>The outdoor air quality monitoring device market encompasses several monitoring types. Active/Continuous Monitoring involves real-time data collection using automated sensors for immediate air quality assessment. Passive Monitoring relies on absorbent materials to collect air samples over time for later analysis. Intermittent Monitoring provides data at scheduled intervals, balancing cost and data volume. Manual Monitoring requires human intervention to collect samples periodically. Stack Monitoring focuses on emissions from chimneys or industrial stacks, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations while assessing pollutants released into the air.</p></p>
