<p><p>The industrial helium purification market encompasses several key technologies. Cryogenic distillation separates helium from impurities through temperature reduction, efficiently producing high-purity helium. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) utilizes pressure variations to selectively adsorb and remove contaminants, making it suitable for medium-scale operations. Membrane separation leverages selective permeability to separate helium from other gases, offering a compact solution. Other methods may include physisorption and advanced filtration techniques, catering to diverse applications and purification requirements in the helium supply chain.</p></p>
