<p><p>The rubber ingredients market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Vulcanizing Agents** enhance rubber strength and elasticity by facilitating cross-linking during curing.</p><p>2. **Protective Agents** safeguard rubber from aging and environmental damage.</p><p>3. **Reinforcing Agents** improve the mechanical properties of rubber, enhancing durability and performance.</p><p>4. **Bonding Agents** ensure strong adhesion between rubber and other materials.</p><p>5. **Others** include various additives that optimize rubber properties for specific applications, contributing to the overall functionality and quality of rubber products.</p></p>
