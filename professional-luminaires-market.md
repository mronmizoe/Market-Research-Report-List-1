<p><p>The professional luminaires market is primarily divided into two types: conventional and LED. Conventional luminaires typically use incandescent, fluorescent, or halogen technologies, providing familiar lighting solutions but with lower energy efficiency and shorter lifespans. In contrast, LED luminaires utilize light-emitting diode technology, offering enhanced energy efficiency, longer durability, and lower heat emission. LEDs are increasingly preferred in commercial and industrial settings due to their cost savings and adaptability, paving the way for innovative designs and smart lighting solutions.</p></p>
