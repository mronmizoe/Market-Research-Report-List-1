<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Frequency Demodulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slope FM Detector</li><li>Coincidence FM Demodulator</li><li>Ratio Detector</li><li>Phase Locked Loop FM Demodulator</li><li>Quadrature FM Demodulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frequency demodulator market includes several key types. Slope FM detectors use the slope of the FM signal for demodulation, while coincidence FM demodulators detect signals based on phase differences. Ratio detectors improve performance in noisy environments by utilizing amplitude ratios, while phase-locked loop (PLL) FM demodulators maintain a stable output frequency in synchronization with the incoming signal. Quadrature FM demodulators separate signals into two orthogonal components, enhancing demodulation accuracy. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
