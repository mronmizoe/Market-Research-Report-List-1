<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Imaging Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems encompass various types, including A-Scan, B-Scan, combined scans, pachymetry, and ultrasound biomicroscope (UBM). A-Scan provides one-dimensional measurements of the eye, primarily for assessing lens and axial length. B-Scan offers two-dimensional images, useful for evaluating retinal conditions and tumors. Combined scans integrate both A and B modes for comprehensive analysis. Pachymetry measures corneal thickness, aiding in glaucoma management. UBM delivers high-resolution, cross-sectional images of anterior eye structures, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
