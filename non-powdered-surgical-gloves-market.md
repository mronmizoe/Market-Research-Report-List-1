<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Non Powdered Surgical Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Rubber Latex (NRL)</li><li>Synthetic</li><li>Polyisoprene (PI)</li><li>Neoprene</li><li>Nitrile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-powdered surgical gloves market comprises various types based on material composition. Natural Rubber Latex (NRL) offers excellent elasticity but can cause allergies. Synthetic gloves, often made from materials like nitrile and PVC, provide better chemical resistance. Polyisoprene (PI) mimics latex's flexibility without the allergens, making it a popular choice. Neoprene gloves provide durability and are suitable for a wide range of applications. Nitrile gloves, known for their puncture resistance, are widely used in medical settings for their protective qualities.</p></p>
