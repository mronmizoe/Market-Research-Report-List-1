<p><p>The Light Gauge Steel market consists of various profiles, each serving specific construction needs. C-Type steel, known for its high strength-to-weight ratio, is commonly used in framing and structural applications. T-Type steel features a T-shaped cross-section, ideal for connectors and support. U-Type steel, resembling a U-shape, provides rigidity and is often used in wall studs and tracks. Other types include L-shaped and Z-shaped profiles, catering to diverse architectural and engineering requirements, enhancing structural integrity and durability in construction.</p></p>
